Zen Qin Orchestra Introduction

The Zen Qin Orchestra was established in 2018. Miss. Lee XunZhi is the artistic director. All members are our class students. The members of the group have achieved excellent results in various guzheng open competitions and examinations. The purpose of the orchestra is to give more young people access to the art of Guzheng ensemble, to let the members develop develop team spirit and a sense of responsibility, to increase the sensitivity of hearing, and to provide art platforms on a regular basis to raise their standard of music performance. Increase the self-confidence and performance experience of the members. In 2019, the Zen Qin Orchestra participated in the Feel the Zen From One Tune, and the performances of the members were well received.
